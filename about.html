About BM Connect

Founded by Bonno Moloi — BM Connect was established to close the gap between off-campus students, lecturers and high-quality housing.

We understand the struggle of finding a place that feels like home. Our mission is to deliver reliable, transparent accommodation solutions that bridge campus life and city energy.

With years of real estate experience and genuine care, our team ensures every tenant finds the perfect match for their needs, budget, and lifestyle preferences.

"We don't just rent properties, we build communities where students thrive and lecturers excel."

— Bonno Moloi, Founder & CEO
