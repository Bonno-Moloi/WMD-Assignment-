❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about renting with BM Connect.

Simply browse our Properties page, find a property you like, and click "Inquire Now" or visit our Contact page to request a viewing. Our team will send you an application form digitally. The process includes submitting identification, proof of income/student status, and signing a lease agreement.

For long-term leases, we require a 30-day written notice before the end of your lease term. For short-term stays (less than 6 months), cancellation is free within 14 days of booking. After that, a cancellation fee equal to one month's rent applies.

Tenants can request maintenance through our tenant portal (login credentials provided upon move-in) or by emailing maintenance@bmconnect.co.za. Emergency maintenance requests are typically responded to within 2 hours, while non-emergency requests are addressed within 24-48 hours.

Yes! We offer flexible monthly payment plans for students. With proof of registration, students can pay rent in monthly installments rather than a lump sum. We also accept NSFAS funding and offer deferment options during exam periods.

Most of our properties include secure parking. Student accommodations typically offer bicycle storage and limited car parking. Lecturer properties always include at least one dedicated parking spot. Check individual property listings for specific parking details.
